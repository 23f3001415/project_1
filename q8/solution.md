# Q8 - Image: The Paradox Portrait

## What you finally submit

You submit **two public URLs separated by one space**:

1. the image URL
2. the JSON URL

The image URL must come first.

## ELI15 idea

This question is not asking for a normal illustration.

It is asking you to make a paradox **feel real**.

That means:

- do not explain the paradox with text
- do not draw a normal chart
- do not show the obvious surface object

Instead:

1. understand the paradox deeply
2. find the strange thing that makes it a paradox
3. build a visual structure that makes that contradiction visible

## What I chose

Paradox:

- `Simpson's Paradox`

Core strangeness:

- each subgroup shows one trend
- but when the groups are combined, the overall trend reverses

That is why I used a **diptych**:

- panel 1 is needed to show the subgroup truth
- panel 2 is needed to show the combined reversal

So here the diptych is not decorative. It is required by the paradox.

## The visual logic

Left panel:

- red and blue streams are separate
- each one climbs upward in its own channel

Right panel:

- the same streams are merged
- the combined flow moves downward overall

So the viewer experiences:

- both local truths are real
- the global truth is also real
- but they contradict each other

That contradiction is the paradox.
